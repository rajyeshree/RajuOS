# RajuOS Vision

## Mission

To build a personal operating system that helps me manage my learning, productivity, projects, and long-term goals.

## Why am I building this?

I want one system that:

- Organizes my studies.
- Plans my day.
- Tracks my habits.
- Helps me prepare for studying abroad.
- Stores my notes.
- Tracks projects.
- Grows with me.

## Long-Term Goal

Build RajuOS into an AI-powered assistant that becomes useful throughout my life.
